Tenant Criteria

WHAT WE REQUIRE

We hold every applicant to the same consistent standard. This protects both you and the property.

📊 Financial Requirements
  • Minimum credit score of 600
  • Gross monthly income = 3× the monthly rent
  • No open evictions in the last 5 years
  • No collections from previous landlords
  • Verifiable income (pay stubs, bank statements, or tax returns)
  • First month + security deposit required at signing
📝 Rental History & Other
  • Positive rental references from prior landlords
  • Valid government-issued photo ID required
  • Background check (criminal history reviewed case by case)
  • Pet policy varies by property — listed on each unit
  • Smoking is not permitted in any BD property
  • All adults (18+) must apply and be on the lease

All screening is conducted in compliance with the Fair Housing Act. We do not discriminate based on race, color, religion, national origin, sex, familial status, or disability.
